Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Zero Knowledge Architecture

1Cryptor is built on a "Zero Knowledge" principle. This means we know nothing about the data you store. We cannot see your files, we cannot recover your passwords, and we cannot access your encryption keys.

2. Data We DO NOT Collect

  • Your Password: It never leaves your device.
  • Your Master Key: It is encrypted on your device and never sent to us.
  • Your Files: File content is encrypted before it ever touches any cloud storage.
  • File Metadata: Filenames and thumbnails are also encrypted.

3. Data We May Collect

To operate the service and improve stability, we may collect minimal, anonymized data:

  • App Store Information: Account status for subscription verification (handled by Apple/Google).
  • Diagnostic Data: Anonymous crash reports and performance metrics to help us fix bugs.

4. Where Your Data Lives

Your encrypted data is stored in your own personal cloud storage accounts (such as iCloud Drive or Google Drive) or locally on your device. We do not operate a central server that holds your files.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at contact@1cryptor.com.
