Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Zero-Knowledge Model

1Cryptor is built so we cannot read your files. Your password and key material stay on your device, and encrypted data remains unreadable to us.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • Your plaintext password
  • Your private encryption keys
  • Your unencrypted file content
  • Your unencrypted filenames and metadata

3. Data We May Process

  • Apple or Google account metadata required for cloud integration
  • Subscription and purchase status from app marketplaces
  • Anonymized crash and diagnostic information

4. Data Storage Locations

Encrypted files are stored on your device and in cloud providers you connect, such as iCloud Drive or Google Drive. We do not host a centralized plaintext file service.

5. International Use

You may use 1Cryptor globally. You are responsible for compliance with local laws related to encryption and cloud storage in your jurisdiction.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, email contact@1cryptor.com.
